-- What is Big O? --

O(1):  Constant Complexity: Constant 常数复杂度

O(log n):  Logarithmic Complexity: 对数复杂度

O(n):  Linear Complexity: 线性时间复杂度

O(n^2): N square Complexity 平 方

O(n^3): N square Complexity  立 方

O(2^n): Exponential Growth 指数

O(n!): Factorial 阶乘
